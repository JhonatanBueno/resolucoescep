<h1>IV - SELEÇÃO E CLASSIFICAÇÃO DOS CANDIDATOS</h1>

<p class=MsoNormal><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt;page-break-after:

10.0pt;font-family:Arial'>Art. 14 </span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial'>Para participarem do processo

uma das matérias que compõem as provas, bem como o mínimo de 12 (doze) pontos
na Redação.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt;page-break-after:
avoid;mso-outline-level:1'><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:
10.0pt;font-family:Arial'>Parágrafo único:</span></b><span style='font-size:

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt;page-break-after:
avoid;mso-outline-level:1'><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:
10.0pt;font-family:Arial'>Art. 15</span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial'> O processo classificatório será
constituido das seguintes etapas:<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt;page-break-after:
avoid;mso-outline-level:1'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:
10.0pt;font-family:Arial'>I - cálculo do total das escores padronizados por
candidato;<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt;page-break-after:
avoid;mso-outline-level:1'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:
10.0pt;font-family:Arial'>II&nbsp;&nbsp;classificação dos candidatos por curso
e turno;<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt;page-break-after:
avoid;mso-outline-level:1'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:
10.0pt;font-family:Arial'>III- desempate.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt;page-break-after:
avoid;mso-outline-level:1'><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:
10.0pt;font-family:Arial'>§ 1</span></b><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Symbol;mso-ascii-font-family:Arial;

mso-symbol-font-family:Symbol'><span style='mso-char-type:symbol;mso-symbol-font-family:

10.0pt;font-family:Arial'> Para o cálculo do total das escores padronizados,
proceder-se-á da seguinte forma:<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt;page-break-after:
avoid;mso-outline-level:1'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:
10.0pt;font-family:Arial'>a) calcula-se o escore padromizado por matéria
através da fórmula:<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt;page-break-after:
avoid;mso-outline-level:1'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt;page-break-after:

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt;page-break-after:

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt;page-break-after:

10.0pt;font-family:Arial'>EP = escore padronizado na matéria;<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt;page-break-after:
avoid;mso-outline-level:1'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:

obtido peIo candidato na matéria;<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt;page-break-after:
avoid;mso-outline-level:1'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:
10.0pt;font-family:Arial'>P<sub>i</sub> = peso da matéria em função do curso.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoBodyText style='text-indent:36.0pt;page-break-after:avoid;
mso-outline-level:1'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt'>b)

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt;page-break-after:

10.0pt;font-family:Arial'>§ 2</span></b><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;

mso-symbol-font-family:Symbol'><span style='mso-char-type:symbol;mso-symbol-font-family:

10.0pt;font-family:Arial'> Para efeito do disposto neste artigo, observar-se-á
que:<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt;page-break-after:

<p class=MsoBodyTextIndent>b) o peso de cada matéria será fixado pelo Conselho

<p class=MsoBodyText2 style='text-indent:36.0pt;page-break-after:avoid;
mso-outline-level:1'><b>§ 3</b><b><span style='font-family:Symbol;mso-ascii-font-family:

<p class=MsoBodyTextIndent><b>§ 4</b><b><span style='font-family:Symbol;
